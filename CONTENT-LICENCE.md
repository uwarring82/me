## Content regimes

The site declares four distinct regimes. The repository's existing [`LICENSE`](./LICENSE) (MIT) continues to govern **code only**.

| Scope | Licence / regime | Attribution requirement |
|---|---|---|
| Code | [MIT](./LICENSE) | Copyright notice preserved. |
| Prose / written content | [CC BY-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/) | Attribution to *Ulrich Warring, uwarring82.github.io/me*, link to licence, and share-alike on derivatives. |
| Portrait `photo.png` | All rights reserved | No reuse without written permission. Personality rights and copyright both protected. |
| Redrawn schematics on publication pages | [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) | Attribution to U. Warring; per-figure caption states licence inline. |

---

## Scope boundaries

- **Third-party content** (publisher-typeset articles, figures originating elsewhere) is governed by the rights of the originating work — not by any regime above. No such content is mirrored on this site. See invariant INV 01 on the canonical policy page.
- **Figures are evaluated panel by panel.** A document-level licence does not automatically clear embedded third-party material. See invariant INV 02.
- **Permission is not obligation.** A paper whose licence *permits* Tier A hosting may still be hosted at Tier C for editorial restraint. See invariant INV 04.
